Persona: Driven Dylan

User Goals

Dylan wants to improve both his athletic performance and mental strength. He is looking for support resources that help athletes manage stress, avoid burnout, and maintain physical wellness.

Current Behaviors

Dylan trains daily with his team and follows workout programs found on social media. When he struggles mentally, he usually keeps it to himself or searches for advice online.

Frustrations

He feels pressure to perform and does not always know where to find trusted mental health resources specifically designed for athletes. He wishes there was a supportive community that understands both the physical and emotional demands of sports.

User Story

Dylan is a 17-year-old high school athlete who dreams of playing college sports. Between school, practice, and games, he often feels overwhelmed. Mind & Motion Athletics provides him with a safe space to learn about mental resilience, recovery strategies, and overall well-being so he can succeed both on and off the field.
